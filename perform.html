Book Us
for a Performance or Workshop

An integral part of Gingarte Capoeira Chicago is the Gingarte Capoeira Performance Company, created in 1996 under the direction of Marisa Cordeiro, an accomplished performance artist who started her professional career with the international performance company Oba-Oba.

Performances are available for a wide range of audiences. They can combine capoeira with another Brazilian cultural dance and they can be as interactive as you want.

Performance rates can occasionally vary, according to the financial ability of the sponsoring group.

Put somewhere: Our performance troupe is known throughout the Midwest. It features Capoeira and other related Brazilian cultural dances and can be customized for your needs. We also offer more casual interactive workshops. Let us bring a thrilling showcase of Brazil to your event!

Please fill out the form below if you are interested in a hiring Gingarte for a performance.

You can also call 312.768.8507 or email us at book@gingartecapoeira.org

Some examples of available performances follow:

Full-Length Cultural Performance

A 45 minute performance including Capoeira and a mix of dance forms in full costumes with live musical accompaniment.

Capoeira and Maculelê

A 20 minute performance including Capoeira and one additional dance form in full costumes with live musical accompaniment.

Capoeira

A 10-15 minute performance of Capoeira with live musical accompaniment.

Capoeira for Young Audiences

A 45 minute interactive performance of Capoeira. Students participate throughout the performance with call and response songs in Portuguese, simple movements, and an opportunity for questions and discussion.

Private Party performance

(rates range from $200 to $3,000)
